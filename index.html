About me

Hi! My name is Joel Plumppu and I am 20 years old. I'm a junior web developer currently studying at Jensens education in Gothenburg, Sweden. I'm currently learning React and Tailwindcss.

Things that I find really interesting are design, psychology and philosophy. With my studies I hope to explore how these different areas can be combined to help people struggling with mental illness.

I have a thirst for knowledge, I always try to find new and different perspectives on things.

In my spare time I like to spend much of my time in nature. I am an adventurer at heart and like to take long hikes in the forest. I also really enjoy listening to music and to cook, preferably at the same time. Whenever I'm not doing any of those things I like to enjoy a board game session with friends.
